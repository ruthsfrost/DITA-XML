How to multiply

Use the calculator to multiply.

Understand What is multiplication?.
  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Choose the asterisk (*) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Choose the equal (=) button.
The calculator displays the result.
You want to multiply 2 and 3. The equation will be 3*2=6. Enter 3. Press asterisk. Enter 2. Press equal. The calculator displays the number 6.
