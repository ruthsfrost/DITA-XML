How to divide

Use the calculator to divide.

Understand What is division?.
  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Choose the slash (/) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Choose the equal (=) button.
The calculator displays the result.
You want to divide 6 by 3. The equation will be 6/3=2. Enter 6. Press slash. Enter 3. Press equal. The calculator displays the number 2.
