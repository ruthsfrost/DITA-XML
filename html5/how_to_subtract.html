How to subtract

Use the calculator to subtract.

To successfully subtract, you must understand subtraction.
  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Choose the minus (-) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Choose the equal (=) button.
The calculator displays the result.
You want to subtract 2 from 3. The equation will be 3-2=1. Enter 3. Press minus. Enter 2. Press equal. The calculator displays the number 1.
