How to add

Use the calculator to add.

Understand What is addition?.
  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Choose the plus (+) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Choose the equal (=) button.
The calculator displays the result.
You want to add 2 to 3. The equation will be 3+2=5. Enter 3. Press plus. Enter 2. Press equal. The calculator displays the number 5.
